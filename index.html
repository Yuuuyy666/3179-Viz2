KTMB in Malaysia

This map visualizes the most KTMB routes by ridership for each rail service line. Each color represents a different category, such as Intercity, ETS, and Komuter.

This line chart shows the ridership trend for five rail service lines — ETS, Intercity, Komuter, Komuter Utara, and Shuttle Tebrau — Q4 2023 to Q2 2025. Overall, Komuter consistently records the highest ridership but shows a decline starting in 2024. In contrast, Komuter Utara demonstrates steady growth, especially toward mid-2025. Other services such as ETS and Shuttle Tebrau remain relatively stable, while Intercity ridership fluctuates slightly at a lower level.

This bar chart shows the highest recorded daily ridership for each rail service in 2025. Komuter stands out significantly with around 92,398 passengers on its peak day, far exceeding other services. Komuter Utara follows with about 16,139 ridership, while ETS, Shuttle Tebrau, and Intercity record more moderate peak ridership levels. The data highlights Komuter’s major role as the most utilized rail service in the network.
